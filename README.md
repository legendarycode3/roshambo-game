Roshambo (ROCK PAPER SCISSOR): </br>
A Roshambo (or Rock, Paper, Scissors) is a simple, two-player, zero-sum hand game used
for decision-making or competition. Players simultaneously form one of three shapes
with their hands—rock (fist), paper (flat hand), or scissors (v-shape)—after counting "1, 2, 3, shoot" or "Roshambo".
